Home Cuisine Facts Habitat The Mantis Shrimp Behold, the mantis shrimp! The mantis shrimp is no ordinary shrimp, and is certainly no ordinary mantis. The mantis shrimp is unique from other ocean dwellers in many ways, such as their fascinating colors to their mating habits. The mantis Shrimp is, without a doubt, the most interesting creature in the sea, and only gets weirder as you learn more about it. Power The mantis shrimp is one of the strongest creatures on the planet in terms of relative strength. Although the mantis shrimp is typically about 3.9 inches long, they can still pack a punch. The mantis shrimp punch is equivalent to the force of a bullet shot from a 22 caliber gun (https://www.kidzone.ws/animals/mantis-shrimp.htm). This punch is great for capturing prey and even breaking aquarium glass! Speed The mantis shrimp not only packs a powerful punch, but a fast one as well. The mantis shrimp can swing at its prey in less than 800 microseconds! To put that into perspective, the human eye blinks in about 350,000 microseconds.
