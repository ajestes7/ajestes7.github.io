Mantis Shrimp Cuisine

If one is ever able to defeat a mantis shrimp, they are known to make great food! The mantis shrimp are oftentimes boiled whole and served on a variety of dishes, such as sushi toppings. The mantis shrimp is apart of many different cuisines, such as the Mediterranean cuisine in Italy and the Asian cuisine in Thailand. Next time you have the chance, dig into some mantis shrimp.
